School

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School (KMGSS)

Manavgyan Secondary School is located in Butwal Sub-metropolitan City, Ward No. 10, Kalikanagar (Sector-1) and Ramnagar (Sector-2), Rupandehi, Lumbini Province. It was established in 1961 A.D (2018 B.S) with the assistance of local residents, many of whom were ex-army personnel. They recognized the importance of educating their children, which led to the establishment of the school in an open ground in the southern part of Kalikanagar. After a few months, the school was relocated to a cow shed before settling in its current location Initially, the school started as a primary school and was later upgraded to a lower secondary level in 2029 B.S. Finally, in 2032 B.S, it became a secondary-level school. Kalika Secondary School provides both general and technical education. In the general stream, we offer classes from Early Childhood Development (ECD) to grade 12. In the technical stream, we provide Computer Engineering courses from grades 9 to 12.
